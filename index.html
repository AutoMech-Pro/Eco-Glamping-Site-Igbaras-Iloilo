Setup Roadmap

The critical steps to launch, from breaking ground to grand opening.

1

Site Selection & Permitting

Month 1-2

Secure land lease or ownership in Igbaras. Begin DENR compliance checks and Barangay business permits. Assess water source availability.

2

Infrastructure & Hardscaping

Month 3-4

Construction of wooden platforms (decks), common area pavilion, and installation of septic tanks/toilets. Installation of solar panels.

3

Soft Launch & Marketing

Month 5

Setup tents and interiors. Invite micro-influencers for free stays. Create social media pages (FB/IG/TikTok). List on AirBnB.
